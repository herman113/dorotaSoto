DOROTA SOTO

Personal Chef

Dorota Soto is a chef, personal trainer and certified nutritionist based in New York City.

Dorota was born and raised in the small beach town of Sopot, Poland. After moving to New York, she became fascinated with health and fitness. She received her certification as a personal trainer and nutrition coach, but she always loved cooking. She continued looking for ways to help people get the most nutrition out of their food and meals.

Dorota strives to create meals that are not only delicious, but nutritious too. As a fitness and nutrition coach, as well as a triathlete herself, she inspires her clients to lead active and healthy lifestyles.

She believes that by making eating healthy as convenient as possible, a healthy lifestyle and fitness goals are well within our reach.
